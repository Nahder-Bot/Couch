<objective>
Unblock the entire existing push stack by (a) wiring the real VAPID public key into `js/constants.js` and removing the `'PASTE_YOUR_VAPID_PUBLIC_KEY_HERE'` placeholder, and (b) adding a self-echo guard to the Cloud-Function `sendToMembers` so actors never receive push for their own actions. These are the two foundational pieces that every subsequent plan depends on.

Purpose: (a) Without VAPID wiring, `subscribeToPush` bails at line 111 and no client ever subscribes — the entire existing scaffolding is dead code. (b) Without self-echo guard, users will get pushed when they themselves scheduled the watchparty / vetoed the title / tapped the approval button. Violates PUSH-05 and is the most-complained-about anti-pattern in push UX.

Output: 1 line added to `js/constants.js`, 1 line changed in `js/app.js` (import path), `'PASTE_YOUR_VAPID_PUBLIC_KEY_HERE'` gone, `sendToMembers` signature extended + 3 existing CF triggers updated to pass actor attribution.
</objective>
