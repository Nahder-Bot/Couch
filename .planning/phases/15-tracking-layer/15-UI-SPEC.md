### Why widget hides instead of showing empty state on Tonight

S1's purpose is to *resurface continuation*, not to teach tracking. A "no progress yet" card on Tonight would compete for attention with couch viz + Flow A entry without giving the user anything to do. When the first tuple lands (via watchparty end or manual mark), the widget appears ‚Äî that's the discovery moment.

### Why D-07 Trakt entry uses secondary button

D-07 explicitly preserves Trakt as opt-in with no friction gate. A brand-grad CTA would imply primacy; `.tc-secondary` matches the "Connect Trakt" posture used in onboarding (Phase 09-07a).
