## Slider step granularity (sport mode)

| Option | Description | Selected |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| a) Logarithmic single slider | Smooth, but value-imprecise (hard to land exactly on 5 vs 6 min) | |
| b) Multi-segment step ramp | 5-sec / 15-sec / 1-min / 5-min / 15-min steps adapt by position | ✓ |
| c) Capped slider + custom picker | Slider only covers 0-30 min; anything beyond goes through Custom picker | |

**User's choice:** b) Multi-segment step ramp
**Notes:** Preserves existing Phase 11 / REFR-10 muscle memory at the live-DVR end (5-sec steps under 1 min) while extending range. Implementation requires non-linear position-to-seconds transform since `<input type="range">` doesn't support per-position step variance natively — researcher to investigate.

## Preset chip ladder (movie mode)

| Option | Description | Selected |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| a) Tight ladder | `Live · 15 sec · 1 min · 5 min · 15 min · 30 min · Custom…` (6 presets, dense at low end) | ✓ (modified — see notes) |
| b) Wide ladder | `Live · 1 min · 5 min · 30 min · 1 hr · 6 hr · 12 hr · 24 hr · Custom…` (8 presets, full range) | |
| c) Hybrid | `Live · 15 sec · 5 min · 30 min · 1 hr · 12 hr · Custom…` (6 presets, samples both ends) | |

**User's choice:** a) Tight ladder + add 1 hour
**Final ladder:** `Live · 15 sec · 1 min · 5 min · 15 min · 30 min · 1 hr · Custom…` (7 chips + Custom)
**Notes:** User explicitly modified the option to add 1 hour between 30 min and Custom. Bridges live-DVR ladder (15 sec - 30 min) into the bigger-late territory (1 hr) without overcrowding the chip strip on phone-width viewports. Custom picker handles 2 hr, 12 hr, 24 hr, anything between.

## Custom picker form factor

| Option | Description | Selected |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| a) Bottom sheet | Slides up from bottom; iOS-native feeling; matches `BRAND.md §7` modal pattern | ✓ |
| b) Full-screen modal | Occupies viewport; matches spin-modal / detail-modal pattern | |
| c) Inline expansion | No modal; chip strip expands into numeric fields when Custom… tapped | |

**User's choice:** a) Bottom sheet
**Notes:** Least disruptive to the live-watchparty UI's layered pattern (lobby card → reactions → footer chips). Reuses existing `.modal` / `.modal-bg` scaffold per BRAND.md §7. Backdrop fades at `--duration-base`; sheet slides at `--duration-deliberate`. The `+5 min` nudge and `Live` reset stay outside the modal (next to chip strip in live UI) so the modal is purely for custom-value entry.

| b) Tonight tab collapsed section | Chevron-toggle section that expands inline (no navigation) | |
