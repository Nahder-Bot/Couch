Couch viz redesign

Five candidate directions for "Who's on the couch tonight?"

What we're rethinking

Phase 14-04 ships couchSize = max(2, totalMembers, claimedCount) capped at 10 → on an 8-member family with 1 claim, you get 1 filled circle followed by 7 dashed-amber + placeholders stretching the screen. User flagged this as "sloppy" and "cluttered."

Dual-mode constraint. Whatever we pick must accommodate both:

1. Proxy-fill — One person physically present marks who's on the couch (e.g. dad at home filling in wife + kids). One operator, multiple seats filled in one session.

2. Self-claim / remote ping — One person remote (e.g. dad at work) wants to see who's down for tonight. Pings family/friends, watches the surface fill in real-time as each person taps in.

VARIANT 1

Density

Slack-style overlapping mini-avatars. Tap any avatar to flip in/out. Single CTA below. No fake placeholders.

✓Self-claim
✓Proxy-fill
✓Remote ping
✗Loses per-cushion metaphor
VARIANT 2

Filled-only + Invite

Only claimed seats render. ONE trailing dashed "+ Invite" cell opens a sheet to mark family in. Vacate via × on hover.

✓Self-claim
✓Proxy-fill (via sheet)
✓Remote ping
⚠Two-tap to seat someone
VARIANT 3

Single CTA

Big pill button flips Find a seat / Leave couch. Roster as thin micro-avatar underline. Most "Apple Health" feel.

✓Self-claim
⚠Proxy-fill (small targets)
✓Remote ping
✗Roster de-emphasized
VARIANT 4

Hybrid

Phone keeps tightened 5-col grid (filled+1 cells). Desktop ≥768px falls to single-CTA layout.

✓Self-claim
⚠Proxy-fill (varies)
✓Remote ping
⚠2 mental models = teaching cost
VARIANT 5

Roster IS the control

Each family member is a toggleable pill. Tap = in/out. Long-press out-pill = send push. One surface, all three modes native.

✓Self-claim (tap your YOU pill)
✓Proxy-fill (tap any pill)
✓Remote ping (long-press)
✓No abstract seats
