# Naming & Trademark Landscape: "Couch"

**Researched:** 2026-04-21
**Project:** Couch (couchtonight.app) — family movie-night PWA, commercial release 2026
**Overall confidence:** MEDIUM (public searches; USPTO TESS and WHOIS behind interactive gates — noted follow-ups)
**Verdict:** AMBER. The name is usable with a qualified brand (e.g. "Couch Tonight"), but bare "Couch" is crowded. See verdict section.

---

**Risk read:** No obvious blocker for bare "COUCH" in Class 9/41/38 from the surfaced data, but "Couch" is a common English word — USPTO examiners often require disclaimers or refuse on descriptiveness for entertainment services (couch = passive viewing). Couch-to-5K brand equity also muddies consumer association.

**Human follow-up required:** paid TESS/Corsearch clearance search in Classes 9, 38, 41, 42 for exact "COUCH" and "COUCH TONIGHT" before Phase 9 commits. ([USPTO trademark search](https://tmsearch.uspto.gov/)) ([Justia — COUCHTOUR.TV](https://trademarks.justia.com/900/57/couchtour-90057206.html))

## 2. App Store Check

**Direct collisions exist and are active.** Multiple shipping iOS apps use "Couch" in the movie/TV space:

- **Couch TV** (Obsessive Software LLC) — DLNA/Plex streamer. [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/couch-tv/id1053712487)
- **Couch Movie** — French TV listings app for cinephiles. [App Store (AU)](https://apps.apple.com/au/app/couch-movie-your-free-film-tv-guide/id1072404497)
- **CouchTimes** — TV/movie tracker with watchlist sync. [couchtim.es](https://couchtim.es/) / [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/tv-show-tracker-couchtimes/id1661813375)
- **Couch (Roku channel)** — [channelstore.roku.com](https://channelstore.roku.com/details/54e2b2ad0cd547d4de2a9b0aaaaa9c83/couch)
- **Couch Movies (Android)** — [AppBrain](https://www.appbrain.com/app/couch-movies-movies-tv/dev.couchstudios.couchmovies)
- **Sofa / Sofa Time** — adjacent tracker apps in the same cozy-viewing semantic space. [sofahq.com](https://www.sofahq.com/) / [sofatime.app](http://www.sofatime.app/)

Bare "Couch" as an App Store title will be **hard to rank** against these incumbents and risks App Store rejection for ambiguity. "Couch Tonight" is materially cleaner — no exact matches surfaced.

**Couch-to-5K note:** Massively known fitness franchise. Users typing "couch" into app search see C25K first. SEO/ASO headwind, not a trademark blocker (different class, different channel).

## 4. International Check

No offensive translations surfaced in primary markets:

- **German:** "Couch" is the same word, same meaning — zero friction in DACH markets. ([Collins DE](https://www.collinsdictionary.com/dictionary/english-german/couch))
- **French:** "canapé" is standard; "couch" is recognized as English loan. The French verb `coucher` (= to lay down / to sleep with) is sexually suggestive in certain phrasings — brand written as "Couch" reads as foreign; unlikely to register as `coucher`, but watch taglines. ([Collins FR](https://www.collinsdictionary.com/dictionary/english-french/couch))
- **Spanish:** "sofá" standard; "couch" is an English loan with no negative meaning. ([Collins ES](https://www.collinsdictionary.com/dictionary/english-spanish/couch))
- **Japanese:** カウチ (kauchi) — pure transliteration, neutral.

**Flag:** The French `coucher` adjacency is the only linguistic watch item. Do not localize the brand to `Coucher`; keep the English spelling. No known slurs or offensive homophones in major markets.

## 5. Verdict

**AMBER — safe to proceed as "Couch Tonight" (or another qualified form), NOT as bare "Couch".**

Reasons:
1. Bare "Couch" App Store search is saturated (Couch TV, Couch Movie, CouchTimes, Couch Movies, Sofa, Sofa Time) — ASO will suffer.
2. "Couch to 5K" owns the "couch + app" mental model in a lot of user minds.
3. Bare "Couch" as a Class 9/41 mark risks descriptiveness refusal; "Couch Tonight" is inherently more distinctive and registrable.
4. couch.com / couch.tv / couch.app are likely unattainable without major spend. couchtonight.app is already owned and aligns perfectly with the tagline.

**Recommended path:**
- **Brand lockup:** "Couch" as the product logo + "Couch Tonight" as the full wordmark and App Store title.
- **File:** COUCH TONIGHT in Class 9 (downloadable app) and Class 41 (entertainment services) at minimum. Consider Class 42 (SaaS) if server-side features.
- **Defensive filings:** COUCH (word mark) in Class 9 only if budget allows — expect examiner pushback.
- **Defensive domains:** grab couchtonight.com, couchtonight.tv, thecouch.app, getcouch.app, oncouch.app.
- **Human follow-up (blocking before Phase 9 commit):**
  1. Paid TESS/Corsearch clearance by IP counsel (~$500-1.5k).
  2. WHOIS on the five domains above.
  3. namechk sweep on @couchtonight across 9 platforms.

### Fallback Names (preserve warm/cinematic/casual vibe)

If counsel flags a blocker on COUCH TONIGHT:

1. **Showtime Together** — family-coordination forward, but "Showtime" collides with the premium cable network. Lower priority.
2. **Popcorn Night** — warm, ritual-y, zero overlap with fitness. Check Popcorn Time association (defunct piracy app — potential brand taint).
3. **Loungely** — cozy, app-y suffix, clean TM landscape likely.
4. **Reel Night** — cinematic, compact, family-friendly. Check against Reelgood.
5. **Snuggle Cinema** — warm/family, highly distinctive. Verify no children's-brand conflict.

Personal recommendation if COUCH TONIGHT blocks: **Popcorn Night** (most intuitive for family movie-night), with **Loungely** as the distinctive-mark fallback.
