v35.1 [deploy date YYYY-MM-DD] · Security & trust

Quiet hardening — nothing to do; a fresh open updates the cached app.

  • Sealed per-couch progress: episode progress, couch nicknames, and "stop nagging me" records are now sealed to their owner per row, not just per family. Nobody can rewrite anyone else's progress or rename a couch they're not on.
  • Pencil hides off-couch: the rename pencil only shows for couches you're on. Named couches now show "Renamed by <name>" attribution — silent vandalism becomes attributable action.
